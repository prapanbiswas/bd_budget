Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026  ·  Effective for: bd-budget.xyz

This platform is a non-commercial, open-source civic technology project. We do not sell, rent, or monetise your personal data in any form. This policy explains what limited information we may encounter during normal website operation and how we handle it.

1. Who We Are

BD Budget Analytics (accessible at bd-budget.xyz) is an independent, non-profit civic technology platform providing free public access to structured analytics on the Bangladesh National Budget FY 2026-27. This platform is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Government of Bangladesh, the Ministry of Finance, or the National Board of Revenue (NBR).

2. Data We Do Not Collect

This is a fully client-side static web application. We do not:

  • Collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information
  • Require account registration or login
  • Store your search queries or filter selections on any server
  • Set persistent tracking cookies
  • Use third-party advertising networks
  • Sell or share any data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Run analytics scripts that fingerprint individual users

3. Technical Data (Server / Hosting Logs)

Our hosting provider may automatically collect standard server access logs as part of normal web infrastructure operations. These logs may include:

  • Your IP address (anonymised at the infrastructure level)
  • Browser type and version
  • Date and time of request
  • Pages or resources requested
  • HTTP status codes

These logs are used solely for infrastructure security, abuse prevention, and performance optimisation. They are retained for a maximum of 30 days and are not linked to individual user identities.

4. Cookies

This website does not set any first-party cookies. Any cookies set by the hosting infrastructure (e.g., for CDN edge caching) are strictly functional and contain no personally identifiable information. No third-party advertising or tracking cookies are used.

5. Local Storage

This application may use browser localStorage or sessionStorage solely to preserve your in-session preferences (such as language selection or currency toggle). This data remains entirely on your device, is never transmitted to any server, and is cleared when you clear your browser data.

6. External Links

This platform contains links to external government websites (mof.gov.bd, nbr.gov.bd) and open-source repositories. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies independently.

7. Children's Privacy

This platform contains no age-gated content and collects no personal data from any user, including minors. The informational content on this site is educational and suitable for all ages.

8. Data Security

All traffic to and from bd-budget.xyz is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Since no personal data is collected or processed, the risk surface is minimal. The codebase is open-source and subject to community audit.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, delete, or export. If you believe any data has been inadvertently collected, contact us at the address below and we will investigate promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related queries or concerns, please reach out via the project's open-source repository or by email at: privacy@bd-budget.xyz.
